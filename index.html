Introduction

Traeceet is designed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our professional movement analysis and pose tracking video app. Our commitment is simple: your data stays on your device, and you maintain complete control.

What Information We Access

Camera Access

  • Purpose: To capture video and detect your body pose and hand movements in real-time
  • Usage: Camera feed is processed immediately on your device using Apple's Vision framework for pose detection
  • Storage: Camera data is not stored separately - it's only used for live pose detection and video recording

Photos Library Access

  • Purpose: To save your artistic pose videos to your Photos library
  • Usage: Only when you choose to save a video after recording
  • Storage: Videos are saved directly to your Photos library under your complete control

Movement Analysis Data

  • What We Process: Body joint positions, hand landmarks (up to 21 points per hand), movement patterns, joint angles, and biomechanical measurements
  • Purpose: To provide professional movement analysis including skeleton overlays, joint angle calculations, movement tracing, and person segmentation
  • Processing: All pose analysis and biomechanical calculations happen entirely on your device using iOS 18 Vision framework
  • Storage: Movement data is processed in real-time and only stored as visual overlays and analysis graphics in your saved videos

How We Use Your Information

We use the accessed information solely for the core functionality of Traeceet:

  1. Real-Time Movement Analysis: Analyzing your movements to generate skeleton overlays, joint angle measurements, and biomechanical assessments
  2. Movement Tracing: Creating visual trails of specific body points over time for movement pattern analysis
  3. Professional Documentation: Combining your camera feed with scientific analysis overlays and artistic visualizations
  4. Video Export: Saving your movement analysis videos with embedded measurements to your Photos library
  5. Customization: Applying your chosen analysis tools, skeleton styles, and visualization effects

What We DON'T Do

  • No Cloud Storage: Your data never leaves your device
  • No User Accounts: No sign-ups, logins, or user profiles
  • No Analytics: We don't collect usage statistics or behavioral data
  • No Tracking: We don't track your activity or create user profiles
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with any external services
  • No Advertising: No ads, no ad tracking, no marketing data collection

Data Storage and Security

Local Processing Only

  • All pose detection and video processing occurs entirely on your iPhone/iPad
  • No internet connection required for core app functionality
  • Your pose data and videos remain completely private to you

Data Retention

  • Movement Analysis Data: Processed in real-time and not stored separately - includes pose detection, joint angles, and biomechanical calculations
  • Videos: Stored only in your Photos library, managed by iOS, with embedded analysis overlays
  • App Settings: Analysis tool preferences, skeleton styles, and configuration settings stored locally in app settings

Data Security

  • All processing uses Apple's secure Vision framework
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • Your data is protected by iOS security features and your device passcode

Your Rights and Control

Complete Data Control

  • Access: View all your videos in your Photos library
  • Delete: Remove videos directly from your Photos app
  • Export: Share or transfer your videos as you choose
  • Permission Management: Control camera and Photos access in iOS Settings

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Videos: Delete from your Photos library
  2. App Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local settings
  3. Permissions: Revoke camera/Photos access in iOS Settings

Revoking Permissions

You can revoke app permissions at any time:

  1. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > Traeceet (toggle off)
  2. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > Traeceet (toggle off)

Note: Revoking camera permission will prevent pose detection; revoking Photos permission will prevent saving videos.

Children's Privacy

Traeceet does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app processes movement and biomechanical data locally for analysis and artistic purposes only. Parents should supervise children's use and manage privacy settings through iOS parental controls.

Third-Party Services

Traeceet does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates independently using only Apple's built-in iOS frameworks.

International Data Processing

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, no international data transfer occurs. Your information never leaves your device or your jurisdiction.

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing is:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing pose detection functionality you've requested
  • Consent: Camera and Photos access granted through iOS permissions
  • Contract Performance: Delivering the video creation service you're using

California residents have the right to:

  • Delete personal information (through iOS settings and Photos management)
